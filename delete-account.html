Important to know

  • Account deletion is irreversible. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use AstroShrine again.
  • Any active bookings will be cancelled and refunded per our cancellation policy before account deletion.
  • It may take up to 30 days for all data to be fully purged from our systems and backups.
  • Data shared with astrologers during sessions (chat messages) may be retained by those astrologers independently; we will remove it from our systems.

Confirmation

After processing your deletion request, we will send a confirmation email to the address associated with your account.
